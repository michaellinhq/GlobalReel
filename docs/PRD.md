# GlobalReel PRD (skeleton)

## Problem
Chinese mid-tier short-drama teams hit a triple wall when going overseas: physical (account bans on datacenter IPs), informational (no trend visibility), operational (no localization pipeline at scale).

## Solution
Three-moat SaaS: physical network arbitrage (SIM + real devices), automotive-grade compliance, full-stack AI + hardware integration.

## Tiers
- **Tier 1 Starter** €899–1299/mo — shared pool, <=3 accounts.
- **Tier 2 Growth** €2499–3499/mo — dedicated device slice, <=15 accounts.
- **Tier 3 JV** 10–20% revenue share — co-operated channels.

## Success metrics
- <0.5% 30-day ban rate on Tier 1+2.
- <€0.80 per localized minute (AI pipeline).
- >€50k ARR by month 6.
